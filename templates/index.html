Aim of this Portal

When we first talk of local governance, the first word that comes to our minds is Panchayat. But the harsh reality is that Panchayats have expanded theoretically more or less in a negative way in today's time. In the appeal to look modern, they have disregarded the grievances of the local public and have often become a political gimmick. When given the opportunity to use tech judiciously, we aim at providing the grassroots level a vivid, easy-to-use website that will become a one-stop shop for all the social and local issues.
